FAQ
How do I reset my streak?
You can use a Streak Freeze (found in the shop) to protect your streak for one missed day. Streaks cannot be manually reset.
Can I change my learning language?
Yes! Go to Settings → Profile → Learning Language. Your progress in the old language is saved separately.
How are XP points calculated?
XP is awarded based on lesson difficulty, accuracy, and speed. Bonus XP is given for streaks and perfect scores.
How do Boss Battles work?
Boss Battles unlock every Sunday if you've completed all daily quests that week. They test everything you've learned in a timed challenge.
